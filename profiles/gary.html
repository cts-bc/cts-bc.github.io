He is a registered Professional Engineer (P.Eng.) in British Columbia, Alberta, Yukon Territory, Nunavut and the Northwest Territories. Gary has over 30 years of experience in transportation planning, traffic engineering and project management throughout Western Canada and New Zealand. His responsibilities include leading technical assignments, presenting findings to municipal councils, mentoring staff and ensuring a high standard of quality on CTS projects.

Gary has worked in both the public and private sector and brings a keen awareness of the needs of both to the table. More recently he has managed a municipal engineering department including all facets of the transportation system. He brings this public sector insight to every project.
